## 📹 Technical Recording Guidelines

### Screen Recording Settings
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- **Frame Rate:** 60 FPS for smooth animations
- **Audio Quality:** 44.1kHz, 16-bit for clear voiceover
- **Recording Software:** OBS Studio or Loom for professional quality

### Visual Guidelines
- **Cursor Highlighting:** Enable cursor effects for clear tracking
- **Smooth Transitions:** Use slow, deliberate mouse movements
- **Loading States:** Let animations complete fully
- **Text Visibility:** Ensure all text is readable at 1080p
- **Color Accuracy:** Test on multiple monitors for color consistency

### Audio Guidelines
- **Clear Narration:** Professional microphone recommended
- **Consistent Pace:** Moderate speaking speed (150-160 WPM)
- **Background Music:** Subtle, healthcare-appropriate music at low volume
- **Audio Sync:** Ensure perfect audio-visual synchronization
- **No Filler Words:** Pre-script to avoid "um," "uh," distractions

### Editing Requirements
- **Smooth Cuts:** No jarring transitions between scenes
- **Highlight Animations:** Subtle zoom or glow effects for key features
- **Text Overlays:** Key statistics and URLs for reference
- **Call-to-Action:** Clear ending with repository link and contact information
- **Subtitles:** Optional but recommended for accessibility

- **Confident**: Convey authority and expertise in healthcare technology
- **Empathetic**: Remember the human impact of healthcare accessibility
